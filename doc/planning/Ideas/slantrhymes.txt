Slant Rhyme Drill

This will be another separate main category of the app, at the same level as Improv Song and Rhyme Drill.

This will be a little different, but similar to the Rhyme Drill.

The user will be given a word from the list of Slide Rhyme Seeds, such as:

wake

The user will then have the option to either see the next word (like in the Rhyme Drill) or see an example of a slant rhyme.
For "wake", the slant rhyme example will be "wait"


These slant rhymes will be separate from the rhyme words.

Initialize the slant rhyme list with 20 slant rhymes.
